Delete Account & Data — FridgePal

Developer: Catalina Avadani
App: FridgePal
Last updated: August 31, 2025

This page explains how to request deletion of your FridgePal account and associated data. You don’t need to be signed in to use this page.

What will be deleted

  • Account data: email and (if provided) name
  • Inventory data: products, categories, expiration dates, quantities
  • Photos: item images stored with your account
  • Preferences: notification and in-app settings

What may be temporarily retained

  • Backups & logs: minimal server backups may retain data for up to 30 days for security and integrity, after which they are overwritten.
  • Legal obligations: if applicable, we may retain the minimum necessary information required by law.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email from the address associated with your FridgePal account to:
    Email: catalinaavadani@gmail.com
    Start deletion request
  2. Include this information:
    • Your registered email address
    • Explicit confirmation that you want your account and all associated data deleted
  3. We will verify your identity via your registered email and confirm deletion.

Timeline

We will delete your account and associated data within 30 days of receiving a valid request. You will receive a confirmation email when deletion is complete.

Questions

If you have questions or need help, contact us at catalinaavadani@gmail.com.

Note: If you submitted this request by mistake, reply to your request email as soon as possible to cancel it (cancellation may not be possible if deletion has already begun).
