Privacy Policy for FridgePal

Effective Date: July 18, 2025
Last Updated: July 18, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to FridgePal! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the FridgePal mobile application (the "App").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your personal information is handled.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use FridgePal, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, first name, and last name
  • Product Data: Information about food items you add to your inventory, including:
    • Product names and descriptions
    • Expiration dates
    • Photos you take or upload
    • Categories and quantities
  • User Preferences: Notification settings, app preferences, and customization choices

Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, features used, and app performance data
  • Technical Data: IP address, device identifiers, and crash reports

Camera and Photo Access

FridgePal requests access to your device camera and photo library to:

  • Take photos of food items for your inventory
  • Select existing photos from your gallery
  • Enable AI-powered food recognition features

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your user account
  • Inventory Tracking: Store and organize your food inventory data
  • AI Recognition: Process photos to identify food items and suggest expiration dates
  • Notifications: Send reminders about expiring food items
  • Data Synchronization: Sync your data across devices

App Improvement

  • Performance: Monitor and improve app performance
  • Features: Develop new features and enhance existing ones
  • Analytics: Understand usage patterns to improve user experience

Communication

  • Support: Respond to your questions and provide customer support
  • Updates: Inform you about app updates and new features (with your consent)

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

  • Supabase: We use Supabase (a secure cloud database service) to store your account information and app data
  • Local Storage: Some data may be temporarily stored on your device for offline functionality
  • Photo Storage: Images are securely stored using Supabase's file storage system

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure user authentication and session management
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Updates: Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • OpenAI: AI-powered food recognition (images are processed but not stored)
  • Expo/React Native: Mobile app development platform

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety
  • Respond to government requests

Your Rights and Choices

Account Management

  • Access: View and download your personal data
  • Update: Modify your account information and preferences
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data

Notification Preferences

  • Control: Enable or disable push notifications in app settings
  • Customize: Choose notification timing and frequency

Data Portability

  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active
  • Account Deletion: When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

Children's Privacy

FridgePal is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Users

FridgePal may be used by people outside the United States. By using our app, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in countries where our servers and service providers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending you a notification if you have opted in to receive them
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
