Privacy Policy for FridgePal

Effective Date: August 31, 2025
Last Updated: August 31, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to FridgePal! This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the FridgePal mobile application (the “App”). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use FridgePal, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address (required). First and last name are optional.
  • Product Data: Information about food items you add to your inventory, including:
    • Product names and (optional) descriptions
    • Expiration dates
    • Photos you take or upload
    • Categories and quantities
  • User Preferences: Notification settings and other in-app preferences.

Information We Do Not Collect Automatically

FridgePal does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, precise location, crash logs, or other device identifiers. Limited technical processing may occur by our service providers (e.g., secure connections and storage) solely to operate the App; we do not use this information to track you or build profiles.

Camera and Photo Access

With your permission, FridgePal can access your device camera and photo library to:

  • Take photos of food items for your inventory
  • Select existing photos from your gallery
  • Enable item recognition features

How We Use Your Information

Core App Functionality

  • Account Management: Create and maintain your user account
  • Inventory Tracking: Store and organize your food inventory data
  • Item Recognition: Process photos to help identify items and suggest expiration dates
  • Notifications: Send reminders about expiring food items (you control this in Settings)
  • Sync: Keep your data available when you sign in on another device

Support & Communication

  • Support: Respond to your questions and provide assistance
  • Updates: Inform you about important changes to the App (where permitted)

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Supabase: We use Supabase (a secure cloud database and storage service) to store your account information, items, and photos.
  • Local Storage: Some data may be cached on your device to improve performance and provide limited offline functionality.

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure sign-in and session management
  • Access Controls: Personal data access is restricted to authorized personnel and services
  • Maintenance: Ongoing security updates and monitoring

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that process data on our behalf and under our instructions:

  • Supabase: Authentication, database, and file storage
  • AI provider (e.g., OpenAI): Optional photo analysis to identify items. Images are transmitted solely to provide this feature; we do not permit use for advertising or independent profiling.

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights or users, or to respond to lawful requests.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access & Update: View or update your account information
  • Delete: You can request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us (see Contact). We will complete deletion within 30 days, unless a longer period is required by law.
  • Notifications: Enable/disable reminders and adjust timing in Settings

Data Retention

  • We retain your data while your account is active.
  • When you request account deletion, we delete your personal data within 30 days (backups may persist briefly for security and integrity).

Children’s Privacy

FridgePal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

International Users

Your information may be processed and stored by our service providers in countries outside your own. By using the App, you consent to this processing.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted in the App and reflected by the “Last Updated” date.
